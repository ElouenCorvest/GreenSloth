Motivation

In 2022 Elouen, the creator of GreenSloth, wrote his Bachelor thesis about comparing the popular steady-state and simple Farquhar, von Caemmerer, and Berry Model and a kinetic photosynthesis model. While searching for the other model, he searched various public sources for scientific literature and tried to recreate several models. During this time he arrived to one conclusion, modelling, especially of photosynthesis, is aimed to help others understand more of the most important process of life on Earth, but the scientific community has not achieved a common way to share their models. This is where the idea of GreenSloth first came in proicien.

Elouen and the maintainers idea and goal for GreenSloth is simple: Create a database of popular kinetic photosynthesis models to make the access to them as easy as possible; That even a Sloth could do it! GreenSloth can be seperated into three important parts of finding models: Searching, Summary, and Comparision.
